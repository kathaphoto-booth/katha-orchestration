The 4-Pillar Heritage Palette

The Rule: No flat hex codes allowed. Color is only applied as physical light, shadow, or oxidized ink.

  • Piña Ecru & Champagne Heirloom: Govern the physical fabric and background light.
  • Volcanic Obsidian: The massive, uncompromising structure of the primary typography.
  • Ancestral Ochre (Vigan Clay): The physical wax seal, used exclusively for the heritage 'K' graft and microscopic actionable accents.

The Typographic Hierarchy

  • The Ancestor (EB Garamond Italic): The historical gold standard of letterpress. Strictly used for the heritage 'K' graft.
  • The Structure (Fraunces): Strictly used for high-impact display mass and architectural gravity.
  • The Storyteller (EB Garamond): Strictly used for narrative body copy.
  • The Instrument (Inter): Strictly used for neutral UI utility and navigation.

The Z-Axis (Physical Depth)

  • Embossed Paper: The L-Frame utilizes complex inset shadows to achieve 3D tactility, simulating paper pressed into the canvas.
  • Letterpress: Typography receives deepened, inverted text-shadows to simulate an antique metal type impression stamped heavily into the fabric.
  • Typewriter Key Physics: Interactive elements depress into the canvas (translateY down) rather than floating, creating true physical validation.
