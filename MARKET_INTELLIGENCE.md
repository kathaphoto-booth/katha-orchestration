# Market Intelligence & SEO Playbook

This document governs the market intelligence acquisition and local SEO strategy for Katha Booth. It outlines Misty Emerson's operational guidelines, the P0 Intent-Precision SEO rule, and SoCal competitor benchmarks.

---

## 1. The P0 Intent-Precision SEO Rule
To protect our bounce rate, organic ranking integrity, and conversion metrics:
*   **The Mandate:** We only optimize for and represent what we authentically handcraft: premium wooden DSLR photo booth keepsakes.
*   **Excluded Zones:** Do not target or optimize for services outside our core identity. Strictly exclude keywords targeting cheap iPad stands, plastic mirror enclosures, or 360-video platforms.
*   **Blog Comparisons:** Educational comparative content (e.g., "Wooden Photo Booths vs. Digital IPad Pods") is permitted, but the main transactional routes must remain focused exclusively on premium physical keepsakes.

## 3. Daily Logging Framework
Intelligence collections are recorded daily in our local market ledger under these distinct categories:

### A. Competitive & SEO Signals (Impact)
*   **Competitor Updates:** Promotional pricing, service expansions, and visual redesigns.
*   **Keyword Ranks:** Local positioning for high-intent queries (e.g., "handcrafted wooden photo booth LA").

### B. Dynamic Operational Costs (Cost)
*   **Fuel Index:** AAA Southern California average regular gasoline price ($/gallon) used to dynamically benchmark logistics.
*   **Labor & Assets:** Setup and attendant compensation adjustments.

### C. Performance & Conversion Signals (Benefit)
*   **Google Search Console:** Monthly impressions, organic clicks, and click-through rates (CTR).
*   **Engagement:** Scroll depth trends and inquiry modal CTA click rates (mobile vs. desktop).

---

## 4. The 30-Day Decisive Loop
On the 30th of each calendar month, Misty Emerson compiles the daily journal entries to generate a **Decisive Market Review**:
1. **Dynamic Surcharge Matrix:** Calibrates a dynamic mileage surcharge beyond our base 20-mile radius strictly mapped to SoCal gas price trends (averting hidden markups while preserving margin).
2. **On-Page SEO Action Plan:** Recommends technical tweaks to page layouts or copy headings to exploit current organic search gaps.
3. **Pricing Tier Calibration:** Assesses package parameters against competitive targets to maximize visitor-to-lead conversions.
