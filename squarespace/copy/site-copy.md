## Page 2: ABOUT / FOUNDERS

### Eyebrow
Founders · Katha Booth

### Headline
Built by siblings.<br>Refined by friction.

### Body
We are two brothers operating two installations across Southern California. One in Carson, one in Long Beach. Different territories, shared discipline.

The wooden booth came first — weathered oak, raw iron frame, DSLR sensor. The white booth followed — clean lines, modern shell, the same uncompromised output. Both print archival cotton portraiture on-site. Both require a trained operator, not a kiosk attendant.

We don't agree on everything. We don't need to. The work holds us accountable.

The name, Katha, is Filipino for "to weave stories together." We treat it as a standard, not a slogan.

## Page 6: FAQ (optional, low priority)

### Headline
Common questions.

**How far in advance should I book?**
60 days minimum. 90+ for peak wedding season (May–October).

**Do you travel outside LA County?**
Yes. Travel fees apply beyond a 50-mile radius from Carson or Long Beach.

**Can I customize the photo strip template?**
Yes. You'll access a private gallery at book.kathabooth.com where you select and personalize your template before the event.

**What's the difference between the two booths?**
Both are DSLR installations printing archival cotton. The wooden booth has a weathered oak frame with raw iron hardware. The white booth has a modern shell with clean lines. Same sensor, same print quality, different aesthetic.

**Do you provide props?**
No. Props dilute the portrait. We focus on lighting, framing, and print quality.

**What if my event is under 3 hours?**
We accommodate shorter events on a case-by-case basis. Inquire with your details.
