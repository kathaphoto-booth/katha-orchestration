# BRAND GENESIS PLAN — KATHA PHOTO BOOTH

> *Katha (Tagalog) — to compose, to invent, to weave a poem or story.*
> Not a noun. A verb. The act of pressing raw earth — fiber, clay, wood — through the patience of the hand until it becomes an heirloom.

This plan is the canonical blueprint for the Katha Photo Booth brand: a narrative-driven identity rooted in the philosophy of *perseverance through the gift of handcrafting personalized passed-down keepsakes*. It spreads Filipino heritage through wabi-sabi web design — timeless elegant poetry, storytelling through texture, flow, and individual creativity, by staying **rooted**.

---

## I. THE STORY (Ingestion First)

### The Word
*Katha* is composition itself. The CITEM **KATHA Awards** (est. 1983) crowned the word as the apex of Philippine material poetry. The original logo, drawn by National Artist **Arturo Luz**, set the typographic north star: **a single continuous geometric line; no excess; "good design is good business."** In 2023, architect Nolasco E. Ditan reinterpreted it as the *Tropeo KATHA* — a stainless-steel trophy bent from a single mono-material line. This is our wordmark constraint.

### The Three Ancestral Parents
1. **Barong Nipis** — the sheer piña/jusi/pinukpok of the Tagalog lowlands. Not a colonial badge but an *ilustrado* canvas: structural transparency, hand-knotted fibers (piña cannot be spun), *calado* drawn-thread openwork, *sombrado* shadow appliqué. **Transparency = refinement.**
2. **Inabel / Binakul** — Ilocos hardwood-loom weaving. *Binakul* is mathematical optical illusion designed to **confuse malevolent spirits** — whirlpools, wind, waves. Apotropaic geometry. The dizzying weave is the household's quiet defense.
3. **T'nalak** — T'boli dream-woven abacá from Lake Sebu, Mindanao. Patterns are not designed; they are received from *Fu Dalu*, goddess of abacá, by **dreamweavers**. Strict tri-color: **Black** (soil, from knalum leaves boiled seven days), **Red** (blood/vitality, from loko root), **White** (purity, raw fiber). Cutting a finished cloth brings illness. When sold, a **brass ring** is offered to appease the spirits — *permission to leave the loom.*

### The Throughline
*Perseverance composed into thread.* Every photo we capture is not a captured moment — it is **a stroke woven into a passed-down keepsake**. The booth is a loom; the print is a *Katha*; the KTHA mark is the brass ring.

## II. VISUAL EVIDENCE (Cosmos Curation)

Direct observation of the `katha filipino` and `barong filipino` Cosmos boards confirms the aesthetic territory and corrects assumption drift:

- **Katha board** curates **objects, not graphics** — Pierre Jeanneret caned chairs, hand-knotted rope daybeds, hanging abacá pendant sculptures, terracotta vessels, weathered oak frames against raw plaster. **The Katha aesthetic is furniture-as-philosophy.** Color story: warm umber → ecru → cement gray → iron-black.
- **Barong board** curates **light through sheer fiber** — cropped close-ups of piña shirts, calado openwork, monochromatic ivory-on-ivory embroidery, lavender shadow-undertones. **The Barong aesthetic is fabric-as-protagonist.**

**Implication for marks:**
- Wordmark "katha" → carved wood object. Gravity. Single-line Luz purity.
- Logomark → loom-frame geometry. Asymmetric. Iron Bark anchor.
- KTHA secondary → piña-sheer. Tone-on-tone. Calado ghost-stitch. The brass ring.

## IV. ASSET GENERATION (Start Up Brand Agent)

**Brief to agent:** Deliver a narrative-driven visual system in three tiers, every tier produced as **(a) high-fidelity SVG + PNG for Next.js portal** and **(b) raster + Squarespace Code Block snippet for storefront.**

### Tier 1 — Primary Wordmark: `katha`
- **Constraint:** continuous-line construction in the Arturo Luz tradition. The mark must be reproducible as one stainless-steel line (Ditan trophy test).
- **Voice:** carved, grounded, asymmetric. Wabi-sabi: one terminal slightly heavier than the others, as if pressed deeper into wood.
- **Primary palette:** Iron Bark on Piña Ecru (light); Piña Ecru on Obsidian Weave (dark).
- **Deliverables:** lockup variants (horizontal, stacked, mono-glyph), clear-space spec, minimum size, dark/light pairings.

### Tier 2 — Logomark
- **Form:** loom-frame geometry abstracted to a single closed glyph. Reads as both **frame** and **kath syllable**.
- **Construction:** asymmetric — one corner heavier (Fukinsei), one edge deckled.
- **Use:** favicon, social avatar, embossed print, booth physical brand-plate.

### Tier 3 — KTHA Maker's Mark (Stamp / Watermark / Secondary)
- **Concept:** the **brass ring** that accompanies a sold T'nalak. *Permission to leave the loom.*
- **Form:** four letters K-T-H-A connected by a single calado-stitch ligature path. Tone-on-tone — ghost-stitch — *sombrado* effect.
- **Use:** corner watermark on every keepsake print, debossed on archival cotton stock, animated as the final stroke of the SVG thread on every digital page.

### Tier 4 — Pattern System
- A subtle **Binakul-derived** background tile (optical apotropaic weave) at 4% opacity, rendered as SVG `feTurbulence` overlay.
- A **calado openwork** divider — the only "rule line" allowed; replaces all `<hr>`.

## V. FRONT END ARCHITECTURE (Front End Design Expert Agent)

### Dual-Track Topology
- **`kathabooth.com`** — Squarespace storefront. SEO surface. CSS-only deckled edges via `mask-image: url(data:image/svg+xml;...)`. Raster sequin background. No canvas. Brand assets injected via Code Blocks and a single brand-tokens CSS file.
- **`book.kathabooth.com`** — Next.js + Vercel + Supabase + Antigravity SDK. Full physics canvas (spring-and-repulsion sequin field), scroll-synced SVG thread weaving via `stroke-dashoffset`, full deckled mask system. Server-side fetches present data as woven narrative threads, never sterile JSON.

### Continuous SVG Thread
A single `Piña Ecru` line shuttles the user down each page — mimicking a loom shuttle or a *calado* needle. Tied to scroll position:
- `O(s) = L · (1 − Φ(s))` where `L = path.getTotalLength()` and `Φ` is normalized scroll progress.
- The KTHA mark draws as the **final stroke** at every page-end.

### Deckled Edges
All image and card containers use a hand-torn SVG mask. **No sharp geometric borders.** Squarespace fallback: pre-rasterized deckled PNG with `mask-image` CSS — degrades to a soft drop-shadow on legacy browsers.

### Sequin Canvas (Next.js only)
Physics-based particle field: spring restoring force + cursor repulsion + sinusoidal opacity for sequin glint. Squarespace surrogate: a 4-second looping `<video>` of the same field at 0.08 opacity.

### Wabi-Sabi Patina Filter
SVG `feTurbulence` fractalNoise at `baseFrequency=0.65, numOctaves=3` applied as a global `::after` overlay at 12% alpha. Works in both stacks.

### A11y Pass (Brock-led)
- WCAG AA contrast verified **at the deckled boundary** (where most masks fail).
- Reduced-motion fallback for sequin canvas and SVG thread.
- Screen-reader narration of the brass-ring KTHA stroke as `aria-label="Katha maker's mark — complete"`.

## VIII. LATEST AGENTIC AUDIT LOOP & CONSTRAINTS

This plan was successfully expanded with the following strict execution rules (sync'd to CLAUDE.md):

1. **The Woven Silk Overlay Rule**: In the React Canvas layer, decorative SVG threads (e.g. raw silk threads, fine champagne lines) must subtly overlay or intrude into the photo slot margins by **12–16px**. Any overlays placed on top of images (`z-10` or higher) MUST use `pointer-events-none` to preserve photo slot interactions.
2. **Accessibility Contrast Rule (WCAG AA)**: Elements on the unbleached ecru paper ground (`#EAE2D5`) must safely exceed WCAG AA `4.5:1` standard. Avoid raw `#9C958A` (Hammered Sequin) on `#EAE2D5` for text; instead, use darkened variants like `#5A564E` or `#6E6A62`. In dark slate cards, brighten text to `text-neutral-300`.
3. **Voice Guidelines**: Technical agentic/SDK terminology (e.g., "Alpha-Transparent Overlay skill", "Antigravity SDK", "automation pipeline") is **strictly forbidden** in user-facing copy. Use Katha's poetic storytelling voice instead ("handloomed artistry," "raw silk fibers," "shimmering gradients").
4. **Local Execution**: The generative design loop (`scripts/katha_design_agent.py`) is fully functional and requires a valid `GEMINI_API_KEY` to run.
