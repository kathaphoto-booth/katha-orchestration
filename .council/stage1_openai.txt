**Verdict:** Launch-funnel-first  
**Reasoning:** Immediate revenue generation is more valuable than delaying the CRM replacement. HoneyBook's ongoing cost during deferral outweighs potential savings. Marketing momentum from a successful funnel can drive long-term growth.  

**Pre-launch Checklist:**
1. Finalize email templates and notify stakeholders.
2. Review and streamline selection API endpoints.
3. Conduct final A/B testing for conversion optimization.
4. Backup and document HoneyBook data flow.
5. Set up initial marketing campaigns.

**Top 5 Conversion Levers:**
1. **Compelling Landing Pages:** Design persuasive, high-converting landing pages optimized for mobile users.
2. **Incentives and Promotions:** Offer exclusive discounts or early booking bonuses.
3. **Social Proof:** Use testimonials, reviews, and social media to build trust.
4. **Personalization:** Implement personalized messaging and recommendations based on user data.
5. **Limited-Time Offers:** Create a sense of urgency with limited-time bookings.
