// Contrast Law — verified, corrected

Capiz Slate (#857D71) passes on Kamagong (4.71:1) but FAILS on cards l1/l2 — inside any container use Rattan (--mut). Achuete is never readable small text — error copy renders in ecru/rattan with an Achuete left-border, never Achuete text. Focus rings are Piña Ecru, not Achuete (Achuete is 2.8:1 on dark — invisible as an indicator). Every pair here has been measured.

Plate 03

Motion — The Quiet-Luxury Law

Motion reads like a solid wood plate turning in soft gallery light: weighted, decelerating, never snappy. Locked and Jed-approved. Try the card — it obeys the law exactly.

Tactile tilt — live

Hover / move over the plate. Max 6°, damped return over 1.2s, power3.out. No spring, no bounce.

katha
Solid wood in gallery light

The law

Every timeline obeys these; every timeline degrades gracefully.

  • Tilt ceiling≤ 6° on any card rotation.
  • Settle≥ 1.2s return-to-rest.
  • Easingpower3.out / power4.out only.
  • Shutterpower4.inOut close→open — preserved verbatim.
  • Forbiddenback · elastic · bounce · spring.
  • Reduced-motionmatchMedia + a branch that force-reveals content. No opacity:0 traps.

The shutter — step device

Plate 07

The Ceiling — Do & Don't

The line between a faithful build and a misinterpretation. Read it as law.

◇ Do

  • Render every tier, price, and add-on from the real catalog — Editorial $949, Glam Editorial $1,149, Architectural $749 (shown unavailable), Katha Booth $549.
  • Carry one shared state through all steps; the ticket shows the client's real name, date, venue, palette.
  • Capture the lead through the serverless function into Supabase; surface every failure.
  • Keep money & legal 100% in HoneyBook via a branded, framed seam.
  • Build the customizer from the real preset gallery + layout registry (lib/layouts.js) — dynamic %-positioned slots, reference-photo upload.
  • Show real Katha photography in the gallery, hero atmosphere, and tier cards.
  • Honor reduced-motion, keyboard, and the corrected contrast pairs.

◇ Don't

  • Use the stale root DESIGN.md — that light "Atelier / Forest + Cream" spec is superseded. This is the only aesthetic.
  • Ship hardcoded demo data (LORENZO / $949 / #8D49F03B) as if real.
  • Fake a submission, a HoneyBook form, or an invoice/contract UI.
  • Render Achuete as small readable text, or as the focus ring.
  • Flatten the customizer to a generic 2-format placeholder, or fill its preview slots with stock (they stay empty for the client's photos).
  • Drop a raw, un-framed HoneyBook iframe that breaks the aesthetic.
