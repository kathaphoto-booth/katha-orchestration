To handle the interim needs using Google Drive/Gmail/Docs/Sheets for client inbox, invoices+payments, and contracts with signatures during the 2-3 week transition until Katha Booth's pb-v3 app ships those features, you can use a combination of existing tools and services. Here are some concrete tools and flows:

### 1. Client Inbox
- **Google Forms**: Use Google Forms to create custom forms for client inquiries and feedback.
  - **Benefits**: Easy to set up and integrates well with Gmail.
  - **Risks**: None if handled properly.

### 2. Invoices+Payments
- **Resend.io**: For email management and tracking.
  - **Integrate Gmail + Resend**:
    - Use Resend to create professional invoices.
    - Use Gmail for sending and receiving emails with Resend tracking.
  - **Benefits**: Professional invoicing with tracking.
  - **Risks**: Ensure privacy settings are configured correctly.

### 3. Contracts with Signatures
- **Adobe Sign (via PDFescape)**:
  - **Process**:
    - Convert documents to PDF using Google Docs or Sheets.
    - Use PDFescape to create and embed signatures within the PDFs.
    - Send signed documents via Resend.io for tracking.
  - **Benefits**: Legal compliance, embedded signatures, easy to track.
  - **Risks**: Ensure contract terms are legally sound.

### Flows and Automation
- **Zapier**:
  - **Integrate Google Drive + Gmail + Adobe Sign + PDFescape + Resend.io**:
    - Automate the following workflows:
      - New form submission in Google Forms triggers an email via Resend.io.
      - Client responds to email, invoice is generated and sent via Resend.io.
      - Invoice with signature request is sent via Adobe Sign (via PDFescape).
  - **Benefits**: Streamlined process, automatic handling of tasks.
  - **Risks**: Ensure proper setup and testing to avoid delays.

### Legal and Deliverability Risks
- **Ensure Privacy Settings**: All emails should be sent from official company accounts and have proper privacy settings.
- **Data Security**: Use strong encryption for any sensitive data and ensure compliance with GDPR if applicable.
- **Compliance with Contract Terms**: Ensure all contracts are legally binding and comply with relevant laws and regulations.

### Summary
By leveraging Google Forms, Resend.io, Adobe Sign (via PDFescape), and Zapier, you can create a zero-cost interim toolchain to manage client inbox, invoices+payments, and contracts with signatures. While there are some risks associated with using free services, following best practices for privacy, security, and compliance can mitigate these concerns.

**Concrete Tools/Flows:**
1. **Google Forms**: Client inbox.
2. **Resend.io**: Email management and tracking for invoices and contracts.
3. **Adobe Sign (via PDFescape)**: Embedded signatures for contracts.
4. **Zapier**: Automation of workflows between Google Drive, Gmail, Adobe Sign, PDFescape, and Resend.io.

**Risks:**
- Ensure privacy settings are configured correctly.
- Ensure compliance with GDPR if applicable.
